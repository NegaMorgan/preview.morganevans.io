FAQ

What do you do?

I'm a software development head. Instead of focusing on that, let me tell you what I believe.

I believe that software is the future in a very direct and tangible way. Progress made today is made on a digital front. Geoff Schmidt put it succinctly in a recent presentation, "software development has become the rate limiting step for human progress."

We live in a pioneering time of digital product development and our actions can have a big reach.

For these reasons, I lead digital product teams. I aspire to make everyone's lives and all digital product interaction better. The best way I can use my skills to do this is by leading technology organizations.

What is your leadership style?

I have excellent people management and coaching skills and am passionate about helping engineers and managers reach their potential.

I evoke a serious craftmanship ethos about coding and design and have an easygoing personality about everything else. I lead by example.

What technologies do you recommend?

It depends on the business needs.

Different technologies work better for different requirements and you have to factor in the starting point and staffing. In general, following XP and Agile methodologies is preferable for sustainability, particularly automating builds and devops.

Personally, I enjoy writing JavaScript and Ruby in my freetime.

Can I hire you to do...?

I enjoy leading engineering organizations where I can influence both technology strategy and engineering process and culture. Appropriate roles might be CTO of a smaller team, VP of engineering or chief-of-staff for a mid-size team, or regional director of a large distributed team.

I also consult from time to time; reach out if you're not sure.
